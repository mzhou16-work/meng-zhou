About

I work under a cooperative agreement between the NASA Goddard Space Flight Center and the University of Maryland, Baltimore County.

My current work focuses on the development of satellite-based fire detection and biomass burning emission algorithms to support the GEOS Earth System Model (GEOS-ESM) and NASA’s FireSense Technology program. I hold an M.S. in Information and Communication Engineering, an M.S. in Informatics, and a Ph.D. in Geo-informatics from the University of Iowa.
